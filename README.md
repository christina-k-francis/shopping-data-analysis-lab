# E-Commerce Sales/Customer Data Analysis Lab

## Analytical Questions
* What are the age ranges of customers who purchase our prodcuts?
* In which regions of the U.S. do our customers live?
* Are there any time-series trends that indicate seasonality in the shopping habits of our customers, such as particular weeks in the year or days of the week? 
* How do our shoppers respond to message blasts (via email or text), pop-up dialog boxes, and webpage banners to promote sales?
* What fraction of advertised-to customers click on links in our promotional content, and fraction of those customers actually make a purchase?
* Are there any garments or products that have shown rising or decreasing popularity over time?
* How much do our customers typically spend for a single order?
