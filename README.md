# E-Commerce Sales/Customer Data Analysis Lab

## Analytical Questions
* What are the age ranges of customers who purchase our prodcuts frequently?
* In which regions of the U.S. do our customers spend the largest amounts?
* Is there a seasonality trend to purchase amounts (i.e. are there times of the year when more people shop or make larger purchases)?  
* Does the use of promo-codes lead to higher purchase amounts?
* Are there any garments or products that have shown rising or decreasing popularity over time?
* How much do our customers typically spend for a single order?
